The past few years have been incredible in the Sigstore ecosystem, seeing Sigstore-signed in-toto attestations be adopted by Homebrew (May 2024), PyPI (November 2024), Maven Central (January 2025), model signing in NVIDIA&rsquo;s NGC (July 2025), and several others.
These deployments make use of great Sigstore features, like the ability to verify content offline, being able to fetch new verification key material with The Update Framework, and the ability to use a tile-based transparency log that&rsquo;s much easier to operate and scale.
