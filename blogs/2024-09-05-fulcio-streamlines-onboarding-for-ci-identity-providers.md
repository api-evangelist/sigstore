TL;DR Fulcio, the Sigstore certificate authority, has introduced a new feature that simplifies the onboarding process for identity providers, mainly for continuous integration. This enhancement eliminates the need for complex, provider-specific logic implementations. Traditionally, integrating a new identity provider for CI involved significant development effort, which required understanding the codebase and cutting a new release.
