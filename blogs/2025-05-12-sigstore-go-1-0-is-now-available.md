We love Go within the Sigstore community, and it’s been our language of choice since we got started. Cosign, Rekor, Fulcio, Policy Controller, and Timestamp Authority are all written in Go, and we’re lucky to have such a vibrant community of Go developers. Cosign was the de-facto Sigstore “client” from the beginning.
